My Passion in IT

Reason

As a kid, I had always been curious about how different things work, how the human body works, how old is the universe. We are living in an era where we heavily relied on technology to satisfy and helps us in our daily life. However, technology and how they work have never been something that interests me as a child, partly might be because it was too complicated for the brain of a young adolescent like me to understand.

When I start the IGCSE ICT class in grade 9, it never hit me off as something appealing. It is not until I’m in grade 10 when I started to learn about basic HTML coding and turtle graphics, that it becomes clear to me how mesmerizing the world of technology can be. I was fascinated by how just a few lines of code can create something so intricate and exquisite, and I become absorbed in learning more about this subject. The A's I received in the IGCSE test in ICT and Mathematics also a huge motivation for me to choose IT as my major in University.

Another driving force for me to follow this path is through various YouTube videos and channels about IT and engineering topics I watch over the years. The channel Code Bullet is my biggest inspiration that led me to become attracted to the concept of artificial intelligence. And the further I research about AI, the more mesmerized I became with this concept and their accomplishments in improving human life.

He lives in an orphan home in a small village deep in the forest. One day, while playing in the forest, the village was attacked by these creatures and his family got killed by them. Out of rage, he swore revenge toward the Dlaejm. Later on, he is taken in by the Grand Master of Yakstuque (a kingdom nearby). Under the Grand Master teaching, the main character starts to train as a knight. You as the player will control him through the training and mission he will receive. Through the mission, you will discover the secret of the Dlaejm. What to do with it will be your decision, and these decisions can affect the course of the story.
